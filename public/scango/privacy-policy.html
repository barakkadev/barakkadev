Privacy Policy — ScanGO

Effective Date: March 09, 2026 Developer: Barakka DEV Contact: barakkadev@gmail.com

1. Introduction

ScanGO ("the App") is a camera-based document scanning application for Android devices. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use the App.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Data We DO NOT Collect

ScanGO does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

2.2 Required Permissions

Permission Purpose
CAMERA Taking photos of documents to be scanned
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Reading images from gallery (Android ≤ 12)
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Saving PDF files to storage (Android ≤ 9)
Permissions are only used for the core functions of the app and are never used for any other purpose.

3. Data Usage

  • Scanned images are stored only in your device's internal storage.
  • Generated PDF files are stored locally and only shared if you explicitly choose to do so.
  • We do not have access to the documents or images you scan.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services which may have their own privacy policies:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google ML Kit Image processing & document detection (on-device) Google Policy
Google ML Kit processes data entirely on-device without sending data to Google servers.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data. Since all processing is done locally, there is no risk of server-side data breaches.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Families Policy Compliance

ScanGO complies with the Google Play Families Policy. This includes:

  • Appropriate Content: All content within this app is safe and suitable for all ages, including children.
  • No Ads: ScanGO does not display any third-party advertisements, eliminating the risk of inappropriate ad content.
  • No In-App Purchases: The app is completely free with no paid transactions.
  • COPPA Compliance: The app complies with the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) — we do not collect personal data from children under 13.
  • GDPR Compliance: The app complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — no user data is collected or sent to external servers.
All data processing is performed locally on the user's device. No personal information is collected, stored, or shared with third parties.

8. Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be notified through app updates in the Google Play Store. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
